AD CLICK PREDICTION¶

The dataset is composed of: -Daily Time Spent on Site: Amount of time in the website -Age: Customer age -Area Income: Average revenue of customer -Daily Internet Usage: daily average time on internet -Ad Topic Line: Text of the advertissement -City: City of the customer -Male: Wheter or not user is a male -Country: country of the user -Timestamp: Time at which consumer clicked on Ad or closed window -Clicked on Ad: 0 or 1 indicated clicking on Ad
